About us

Apartment Jelena has been entertaining its loyal guests for twenty-five years and has become synonymous with family vacations and socializing. If you have not visited us yet, let us surprise you with our kindness and simplicity and offer you a quiet accommodation that exudes relaxation and peace of mind. Our guests are always satisfied with the care of Mrs. Jelena Pericic, who has been renting apartments as a private family business for over 50 years. Whether you want to spend your vacation in a family atmosphere or simply looking for some peace and quiet to “recharge” and enjoy the beauty of our Kvarner, you are in the right place. Give us your trust and we will not let you down.



About the owner

Owner Jelena Pericic, born November 2, 1951, has been caring for her guests for many years and never misses anything. He understands the needs of his guests and strives to fulfill all their expectations. She built the apartments she owns with her late husband Nikola Pericic. She is always on the move, but in the evening she loves to rest while watching her favorite Turkish series. In addition to running an apartment in her spare time, she likes to go mushrooming or traveling the world with her sisters. She loves to cook and bake cakes for her guests, which is why her house always smells of delicious delicacies, but don't worry, there is no GMO in her kitchen, everything is 100% homemade and fresh.
